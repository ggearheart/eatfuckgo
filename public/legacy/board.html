🎲 Movement Rules (Titan)
1. Split (optional). Before rolling, you may split one stack into two (each keeps ≥2 cards).

2. Roll 1d6. You may move some or all agents, but must move at least one if able. Each moves exactly that many pieces — the full count. First turn: you may re-roll once (the second roll is binding).

3. Follow the Signs:
▸ Arrows — direction of travel. May start this way, and a stack passing through must keep going along the arrows (no backtracking).
◖ Arch — you MAY begin a move this way (first step only).
◢◢ Block — you MUST begin a move this way — it's the only path off that piece (first step only; ignored when passing through).
Blocks & Arches govern only your starting piece. Arrows govern both the start and every step after.

4. Ending a move. You may move through your own stack but not end on it. Land on an enemy → battle (move stops there). No moving into or across a piece already in battle. A roll of 6 also permits teleportation.
